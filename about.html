About Esports

Esports (Electronic sports) is a competitive sport where gamers use their physical and mental abilities to compete in certain genres of video games in a virtual, electronic environment. These esports video games titles can be played across electronic devices (PC, Mobile, Consoles, unmanned vehicle, unmanned aerial vehicle, robot, simulation etc.)

The video game genres associated with esports are: Real-time Strategy (RTS) e.g., Hearthstone Fighting e.g., Tekken, Street Fighter Multiplayer Online Battle Arena (MOBA) e.g., DOTA2, League of Legends Sports e.g., FIFA, Racing First-person Shooter (FPS)/Battle Royale e.g., Counter Strike, Call of Duty, BGMI

Esports is a Sport and hence, it has received its sporting recognition from the International Olympic Committee (IOC), which is the apex body of sports along with Olympic Council of Asia (OCA), Commonwealth Games etc.
